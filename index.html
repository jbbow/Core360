  • betterHOW?

    The main purpose of core360® is to facilitate good muscle contraction and activity. It assists in the proper re-education of the neuromuscular movement patterns or pathways in our brain that dictate the proper coordination, quality and timing of muscle activity. Improving these patterns improves movement, stability, strength, and function*.

    • Abdominal bracing/contraction
      The user can apply 2-6 pieces and locate them in various positions along the main band. Typical placement is for the diaphragm retraining. The user then actively contracts or braces their abdominal muscles by pushing out into the pieces.

    *This is NOT a passive brace meant to stabilize the back or passively improve posture. It is meant to facilitate better active contraction and coordination of the abdominal wall/muscles
